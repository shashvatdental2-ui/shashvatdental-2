Implants and Crown

Orthodontic Care

Wisdom Teeth Removal

Meet Our Team

Dr. Lipsa Shah Doshi

Pediatric Dentist & Interceptive Orthodontist

With a gentle touch and a passion for children's oral health, Dr. Lipsa specializes in providing compassionate dental care for our youngest patients. She is an expert in early dental development and uses interceptive orthodontics to guide healthy, beautiful smiles from an early age.

Dr. Darshan Doshi

General Dentist, Implantologist, FMR Expert

Dr. Darshan is dedicated to comprehensive family dental care. As a skilled implantologist, he provides advanced solutions for tooth replacement, and his expertise in FMR (Full Mouth Rehabilitation) ensures a holistic approach to restoring both function and aesthetics for adults of all ages.
